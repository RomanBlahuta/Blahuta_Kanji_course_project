Basic Information

This web application will help you learn basics about hiragana, katakana and kanji!

KanjiLearn provides its users with interactive learning games for japanese language!
This app allows you to learn japanese syllabaries hiragana and katakana by going through
interactive quizes. There is also an option of learning these two kanas simultaneously!
This method is even more effective because it helps to associate the characters from one kana
with the characters from the other one.

The main purpose of this app is to help with learning kanji - japanese hieroglyphs.
KanjiLearn offers two ways of learning kanji: you can either learn them by manually searching
for their meanings and other details, or you can add hieroglyphs to your 'to learn' list and
play a learning mini-game based on this list. Kanji learning will be focused on kanji meaning,
its structure and radicals.
